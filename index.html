Challenging Star Darshan

Born: February 16, 1977, in Ponnampet, Karnataka, India

Occupation: Actor, Producer, Distributor

Darshan Thoogudeepa, popularly known as DBoss, is one of the biggest stars of Kannada cinema. Known for his mass appeal, action roles, and deep voice, he has a massive fan base across Karnataka. He started his career in 2001 and became a top hero with films like Kariya, Saarathi, Yajamana, and Kurukshetra.
